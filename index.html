Explaining the visual

In this visual you can see the 3 cocktails separated in their own column. Every individual dot, dancing in the visual, represents one emotion form one participant. This means that every participant has 7 dots dancing around. The higher the dot, the stronger the emotion at that given time point. The time slider makes it possible to see the change of the emotions through the experience of the cocktail. The experience includes smelling the cocktail and drinking the cocktail.

It is possible to pause and play the time. To hover over the dots which gives more information about that specific dot. And it is possible to click on a dot. Clicking on a dot directs you to another page in which a new visual is represented. Finally, it is possible to select and deselect different filters, changing the visual.

Explaining the visual

In this visual you can see the 3 cocktails separated in their own row. The width of the color represents the average amount of that emotion measured across all participants. The colors representing the emotion are the same as the colors in the visual above.
