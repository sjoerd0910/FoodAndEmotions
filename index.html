Explanation

In this visual you can see the 3 cocktails separated from the TasteLab taseting sessions, each in their own column. Every individual dot, dancing in the visual through time, represents one emotion form one participant. This means that every participant has 7 dots per cocktail representing them. The higher the dot is located on the page, the stronger the emotion at that given time. The time slider makes it possible to see the change of emotions from the participants while experiencing the cocktails. The experience includes smelling and drinking the cocktail.

To get more information about a certain participant, just hover your cursor above the dot you want to inspect. This will show the participant, their age and gender. To get even more information about that participant, you can click on a dot. This will bring you to a new page with a new visual representation from that participant. It is also possible to select and deselect different filters such as emotions, gender, or age, which changes the scope of the visual.
